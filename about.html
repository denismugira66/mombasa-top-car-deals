About Our Platform

Mombasa's Top Car Deals is Kenya's leading automotive marketplace, connecting buyers, sellers, dealers, and service providers across the nation. Founded in 2020, we have facilitated over 1,000 successful vehicle transactions.

Our mission is to create a trusted, transparent, and efficient automotive ecosystem that benefits everyone - from first-time car buyers to experienced dealers and automotive professionals.
